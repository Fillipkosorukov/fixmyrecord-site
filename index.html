Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a background check?

No. FixMyRecord is not a consumer reporting agency. We scan publicly available information to show you what others can find about you online.

Will my employer see that I ran this scan?

No. Your scan is completely private. We don't share your information with anyone.

How long do removals take?

Most data broker removals process within 1-4 weeks. Some sites take longer. With the $19/month monitoring plan, we track progress and re-submit if needed.

Expunged and dismissed records often still appear on third-party sites. Our scan finds these and the Action Plan includes dispute letters citing your state's expungement laws.

Is my information safe with FixMyRecord?

We use encrypted connections and don't store your personal information beyond what's needed to run your scan and deliver your report.
